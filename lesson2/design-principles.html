Proximity

Crumble

Visit Crumbl Cookies

The website for Crumbl is a great example of proximity. There is not any visible padding between the different elements of the page. The image is touching both the header and the footer of this page.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

Visit Apple

Apple's website is the perfect example of the proper use of white space and clean design. Thier website has an enormous amount of white Space which gives it a simple and clean design. Because there is so much white space on this website your eyes are dawn to the product images, which is what they want you to look at.

Visual Hierarchy

Hello Fresh

Visit Hello Fresh

Hello Fresh's website demonstrates Visual hierarchy. The main photo on the page even follows the visual hierarchy principle. Their are food of various shapes and sizes. There is also visual hierarchy with the text on this page. For example, they made the word money bigger than all the other words so that it would stand out more to the viewers.
