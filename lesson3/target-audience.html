Target Audience

The audience for this website are going to be travelling throughout Utah. This includes:

  • Residents
  • Tourists
  • Travelers

Personas

  • Martha is a computer engineer. She recently moved to Wyoming. There is a family gathering in a few days in her hometown in Utah. Flying with her family is not an option, but she knows how unpredictable the weather can be in Utah. Martha knows she should look at the weaher for the day they are leaving. She does not have a phone, but has an Ipad that shes uses as a phone.

  • Scott is a college student attending Brigham Young University in Rexburg, Idaho. He is in his Winter Semester. His family lives in Logan, Utah. He visits his family every other Saturday. He knows how unpredictable the Weather in Utah can be, but he is supposed to visit his family this weekend. He mainly uses his phone to look things up.

Senarios

  • Hyrum has an interview 2 hours away from his home in Richmond, Utah. He wants to plan when he needs to leave in order to get to the interview on time. However, he needs to plan for the weather and plan more time if there will be bad weather. He visits utahweather.com to plan out his trip.

  • Elese is wanting to attend a quilting class next week. It is only 20 minutes away from where she lives now. Her car does not drive well in the snow and doesn't want to be late to the class. She visits utahweather.com to plan when she will need to leave for her class to make it on time.

  • Brenda has a family party in Saint George, Utah this weekend. She is in charge of a few activities and can't miss. She is trying to determine if she should leave Friday afternoon, or Saturday early morning. She visits utahweather.com to help influence her decision.

  • Kalvin's grandfather recently passed away due to Covid-19. He is going to attend his funeral in Utah, but he lives in Arkansas. He wants to know what he should pack depending on what the weather will be like. He visits utahweather.com to find this information so he is able to pack accordingly.

  • Kaitlin has to commute for her job in Layton, Utah. It is 45 minutes from her house. She needs to know when to leave for work so that she won't be late. She visists utahweather.com to check on the weather for the days she works next week.
